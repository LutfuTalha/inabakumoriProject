About

Inabakumori is a Japanese musician, Vocaloid producer and songwriter. His most notable works are Lost Umbrella (ロストアンブレラ) in 2018 and Lagtrain (ラグトレイン) in 2020

Career

Inabakumori began publishing music in 2016, debuting with the song Secret Music (秘密音楽) using Kaai Yuki. His content surged in popularity some time after the release of Lost Umbrella (ロストアンブレラ)[2] in 2018, when the song was used widely on TikTok. He published his first album ANTICYCLONE (アンチサイクロン) in 2019.


His early songs featured Kaai Yuki, drawn by himself, but his later songs featured the character Osage-chan. The art for his videos was done by himself until the release of "Lost Umbrella", where he collaborated with illustrator Nukunukunigirimeshi.
